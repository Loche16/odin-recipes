Thai jasmine rice

Description

A simple and good side dish for the most various meals.

Ingredients

  • half of a small mug with thai jasmine rice;
  • 1 small onion;
  • 1 clove of garlic;
  • OLive oil;
  • A full small mug of water.

Steps

  1. Start by adding olive oil to a pan and the onion. When the onion is translucent add the garlic (Don`t forget to mix);
  2. After 3 minutes add the rice, close the pan with a lid and let the rice cook for about 15 minutes.
